Who are we?

We are Project Centams! A group of talented first-year IT-AGD students embarking on a mission to make a positive impact. With their unwavering dedication and creativity, they aim to raise 6 Thousand Pesos for charity through the sale of their meticulously crafted designs and products. Inspired by their shared passion for giving back, these aspiring technologists are ready to combine their IT expertise with their artistic prowess to bring meaningful change to their community.

How it started

On April 23, 2023, we were tasked to group ourselves for our NSTP Project so we decided that we should create a group since we were all close friends. We received instructions from our NSTP Professor, Sir Adrian Duque, that we are required to raise 6 Thousand Pesos to pass the subject. How we're gonna achieve that is up to us. Not long after finalizing our group, we immidiately went to deciding what to sell. We came to the conclusion that we're going to sell t-shirts, tote bags, and pins with self-made designs, on top of all that we decided to also set-up digital art commissioning to put our talents to use. So then begins our journey of hardship and complications.

Our Specialty?

A lot of the competition actually sells mostly similar things, however, our products are 100% self-made designs, all made from scratch, pure skill. In every design, we revolve around one or multiple original characters of ours, namely Fintam the human, JSON the robot, Ovin the living pixel, and Ice Cream. Each character has their own personality and charm, take that along with cutely drawn designs. Lastly, we believe we are one of the only groups to offer commissions for digital art, costumers ask descirbe what they want us to draw, and we provide it for them!

Project Centams

Project Centams was created by a group of nine people with the objective of establishing a business and raising funds for the NSTP requirement. These individuals were tasked from the start with figuring out how to cooperate successfully and build a successful business.
